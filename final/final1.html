Yes, I know that enthalpy is such a weird word but what can we do to reduce the wierdness of this word?

Enthalpy is actually a common word used in thermodynamics, specifically chemistry.

Definition

Enthalpy is defined as a sum in a system's total internal energy.

Uses

Enthalpy is used in a variety of different problems, such as Hess's law, heats of formation and reaction, and much more.
