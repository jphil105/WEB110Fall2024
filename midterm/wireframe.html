Planned Structure

This website will focus on raising awareness of a high-grade malignant brainstem tumor present in children called DIPG (diffuse intrinsic pontine glioma).

This website will include pictures and information about the children who fell victim to DIPG and how they lived throughout their lives.

The colors of this website will represent hope, awareness, and kindness (see color psychology for more information).
